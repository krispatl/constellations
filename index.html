CONSTELLATION LABS

Shared Infrastructure • Digital Culture • Catalonia
Mission Program Open-Source Impact Connect
Active infrastructure / non-commercial / sector articulation

A shared lab to strengthen Catalonia’s digital culture sector.

Constellation Labs is a permanent platform for advanced research, learning, and collaboration at the intersection of art, science, and emerging technologies — based at Espronceda and developed by IMMENSIVA × NUBIA.

Focus XR • AI • Robotics • Creative Coding
Format Open Labs • Hack-Labs • Knowledge Exchange
Core output Open-source repository

Mission

          
Why it exists / what it solves

        


        

          

            
Structural need

            

              The rapid acceleration of AI and immersive technologies is reshaping cultural production,
              but the sector lacks continuous shared infrastructures for collective experimentation, reflection, and coordination.
            

            

              sector fragmentation
              access barriers
              missing connective tissue
            

          


          

            
Operating model

            

              Not a coworking space. Not isolated events. Constellation Labs is a permanent articulation platform
              that prioritizes collective learning, critical engagement, and durable networks over individual production outcomes.
            

            

              continuous lab
              P2P + B2B
              knowledge transfer

Program

          
A functional system, not a one-off event

        


        

          

            
01Open Constellation Labs

            

              
Facilitated open-access lab sessions

              
Guided access to shared tools and collaborative workflows for experimentation and peer learning at Espronceda (Barcelona). Continuous operation.

            

          


          

            
02Hack-Lab Sessions

            

              
Time-bound collaborative research labs

              
Multidisciplinary labs focused on developing tools, workflows, and conceptual approaches at the intersection of art, science, and technology.

            

          


          

            
03Knowledge Exchange

            

              
Talks, working groups, semi-public sessions

              
Critical and creative engagement with emerging technologies in cultural contexts, designed for knowledge transfer and sector coherence.

            

          


          

            
04Sector Connectivity

            

              
P2P + B2B collaboration development

              
Matchmaking sessions, informal networking, and exploratory partnerships connecting cultural entities, academic partners, and industry stakeholders. Hybrid format.

            

          


          

            
05Documentation Outputs

            

              
Open documentation + sector publications

              
Production of shared documentation, methodological insights, and sector-relevant outputs disseminated through digital channels.

Impact

          
Professionalization / cohesion / cross-sector spillover

        


        

          

            
Professionalization + sustainability

            

              Shared access to advanced technologies and structured research environments improves professional capacity,
              reduces access barriers, and supports sustainable collaboration models.
            

          


          

            
Sector cohesion + networks

            

              A stable convergence point connecting dispersed actors and linking Catalonia to national and international
              cultural, academic, and technological networks.
            

          


          

            
Cross-sector spillover

            

              Generates impact across higher education, scientific research, creative industries, innovation ecosystems,
              and social impact initiatives — rooted in culturally grounded technology development.
            

          


          

            
Visibility + dissemination

            

              Communication emphasizes sector-level impact and shared knowledge through open documentation,
              publications, and coordinated dissemination via Espronceda / IMMENSIVA / NUBIA channels.
